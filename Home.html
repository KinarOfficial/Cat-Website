My Background

I have moved around the majority of my life being an army brat and finally settled in North Carolina. I attended Crest Middle school then went on to Cleveland Early College High School or CECHS where I graduate from high school and CCC at the same time. After graduating from CCC I went on to UNCC pursuing a B.S. in Game Development and Design and Artificial Intelligence. After completing the majority of the program I left UNCC and attended again CCC pursuing their Web Dev and Design degree as well as Networking and Cybersecurity degrees. However, jumping to more personal levels, I typically find myself playing and streaming games. I adore building and maintaining PCs, setting up devices especially Smart devices, and generally making PC setups look appealing through dusting and cable management.
